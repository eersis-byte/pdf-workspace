About PDF Workspace

A free, privacy-first suite of 47 PDF tools that run entirely in your browser.

🔒 Privacy Promise

Every file you process stays on your device. We have no servers that receive your data — all PDF manipulation happens client-side using JavaScript. We cannot see, store, or access your files.

📡 How Libraries Load

The JavaScript libraries that power PDF processing (PDF.js, pdf-lib, JSZip, etc.) are loaded from public CDNs on your first visit. After that, the service worker caches them locally and the app works fully offline. Your PDF files are never sent anywhere.

🍪 No Cookies, No Tracking

PDF Workspace uses localStorage only for your preferences (dark mode, favorites, tool settings). There are no analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting, and no advertising.
